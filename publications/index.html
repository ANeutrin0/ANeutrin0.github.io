FAIR: Framing AI's Role in Programming Competitions - Understanding How LLMs Are Changing the Game in Competitive Programming.
CHI 2026, co-second author.

This work studies how large language models are reshaping competitive programming ecosystems. My contributions include collecting and analyzing large-scale Codeforces contest data from 2022 to 2025, designing behavior-based evidence analysis for hidden AI use, and conducting a semi-structured interview with an ICPC World Finals champion.
