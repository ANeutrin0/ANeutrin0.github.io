I am an undergraduate student in **Computational Communication** at the **Communication University of China**, expected to graduate in June 2027. I am currently a research intern in the **Social Computing Group at Microsoft Research Asia**, working on LLM agents and AI-supported knowledge workflows.

My interests sit at the intersection of **social computing**, **computational communication**, **human-computer interaction**, and **AI agent systems**. I am especially interested in how large language models can be evaluated, coordinated, and used to support research and decision-making in socially meaningful contexts.

Research Interests
======

- LLM agents and tool-using AI systems
- LLM-as-a-judge and evaluation workflows
- Social computing and computational communication
- Human-computer interaction
- AI-supported scholarly search and evidence synthesis

Selected Highlights

Recent Work
======

At Microsoft Research Asia, I contribute to **Copanion**, an AI agent framework for task decomposition, tool orchestration, retrieval-augmented reasoning, evidence synthesis, and report generation. Based on this framework, I lead the design and implementation of **TalentScope**, an academic talent discovery system for candidate identification, research trajectory analysis, publication review, and research-fit assessment.
